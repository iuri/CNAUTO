Product - Sigma Grid - Sample

Combination filters

This sample demonstrates how to apply several filters to one grid.

  • Input your criterias for employee name in the first line.
  • Input your criterias for bill of 2005 in the second line.
  • Press button titled filter to see what happened.
  • Press button titled Unfilter to restore and show all the records.
